 So, after a bunch of same Goukis, i started (and finish) a Gouki with SSF IV moves and old design
 What you guys guess about?

 [spoiler]
http://g.imagehost.org/0488/ShinGouki.png[/spoiler]
 
 How you guys can see, is the colored sheet of this game, with some editt and extra frames made by myself

 MOVES
 - All the SSF4 commom moves like HK with 2 hits, frontal Low HP,  Low HK like in SF3 and etc
 - Throw move like in SSF 4, grab and smash with palm

 SUPER MOVES
 - Gou Hadouken, Zanku Hadouken, Shakunetsu Hadouken (fireballs): common, like in the others games, single hit and 3 hit to shakunetsu
 - Gou Shoryuken (anti-air): 3 hits, no fire, no paint effects
 - Tatsumaki Zankukyaku (hurrikane kick): 2 hits, to Juggle and with the wind around like in SSF4 (can be done in the air)
 - Hyakki Shuu (air flip): can be finished like in SSF 4: direct punch, dive kick and slide(showing back)or throw over the head.
 - Ashura Senkuu: with eyes glowing red
 - Dive kick: showing front, same effect

 Super Combos (Level 1)
 - Messatsu Gou Hadou (super fireball): Like in SF3 3rd Strike, in the air is Tenma Gou Zankuu and in the floor projectile 8 hits, but always single fire ball (as finish air combo, multiple fireballs)
 - Wrath of Raging Demon (Shun Goku Satsu Ultra I): Like in SSF IV will hit 27 hits, but don't will damage too much. All the frames and Kanji to this is on the Sprite sheet, and fonectally Gouki says "I - SHU - SUN GEKI".

ULTRA COMBO (Level 3)
 - Demon Armageddon (Tenshou Kaireki Jin): Like in SSF IV Gouki make a cool and violent kick that launch the foe on the air, but using the same screen, won't gonna be like a "cutscene skill". Still miss a frame, the first to the dinamic stance
